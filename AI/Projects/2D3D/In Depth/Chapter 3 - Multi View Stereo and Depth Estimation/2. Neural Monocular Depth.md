# 2. Neural Monocular Depth

To fix the flaws of classical MVS (failing on blank walls and reflections), your pipeline utilizes deep learning—specifically **Depth Anything V3**.

## Monocular Priors
A monocular depth estimator looks at a single 2D image and guesses the depth without stereopsis. It does this by leveraging "Priors" (learned context):
*   It knows that things lower in the frame are usually closer.
*   It knows that humans are roughly a specific size.
*   It understands atmospheric haze.
*   It predicts absolute scale directly from pixel context, rather than relying strictly on triangulation angles.

By processing individual video frames through `DepthAnything3.inference()`, the pipeline guarantees perfectly filled, hole-free depth maps even over challenging, textureless walls.
