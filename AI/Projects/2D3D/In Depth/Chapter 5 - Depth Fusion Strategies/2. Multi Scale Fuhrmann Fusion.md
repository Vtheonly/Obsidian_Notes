# 2. Multi Scale Fuhrmann Fusion

Standard TSDF fails when mixing close-up macro shots with distant wide shots. The low-resolution data from the distant camera will mathematically "blur" and destroy the intricate high-resolution data from the close-up camera.

Your pipeline offers an alternative strategy: **Multi-Scale Adaptive Fusion** (Based on Fuhrmann et al., 2011).

## The Strategy
1.  **Scale Band Sorting:** For every point extracted from a depth map, the algorithm calculates its distance to the camera center using $L2$ Normalization.
    *   Close points $\rightarrow$ FINE bucket.
    *   Mid points $\rightarrow$ MEDIUM bucket.
    *   Far points $\rightarrow$ COARSE bucket.
2.  **Native Downsampling:** Each bucket is voxel downsampled using a size matched to its native optical resolution. The FINE bucket might be voxelized at 1.5 cm, while the COARSE bucket is voxelized at 8 cm.
3.  **Adaptive Union:** The cleaned buckets are merged (`+=`) into a single unstructured point cloud.

This ensures that wide-shot architectural structure is maintained without sacrificing the high-resolution geometry of doorways and window frames.
