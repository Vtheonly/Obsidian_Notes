
# 1. Curriculum Learning Strategy

Training a 6.2M parameter MVS network requires strict staging. If you immediately feed it chaotic, massive 100k real-world city photos, the weights will destabilize, confusing infinite sky with glass reflections. 

The pipeline mandates a **6-Stage Curriculum Learning Strategy**:

1.  **Stage 1: DTU Dataset (Physics of Depth):** Indoor, laboratory scenes with robotic camera precision. Trains the core stereopsis logic without domain noise.
2.  **Stage 2: WHU-MVS (Synthetic Architecture):** Flawless synthetic aerial city datasets. Teaches the network strict architectural constraints (90-degree corners, flat roofs).
3.  **Stage 3: BlendedMVS (Domain Transfer):** High-quality real-world drones over ruins and structures. Bridges the gap between synthetic perfection and real-world lighting/shadows.
4.  **Stage 4: Custom 100k Dataset (Monocular Priors):** Ingests pseudo-labeled data (via DepthAnythingV3). **Rule:** Freeze the 3D-UNet to maintain metric accuracy, training only the 2D ECA-Net backbone to memorize specific city textures.
5.  **Stage 5: UrbanScene3D (Specialization):** Teaches the model to resolve extreme scale and slice through physical occlusions (like trees in front of walls).
6.  **Stage 6: Tanks and Temples (The Final Polish):** Executed at a tiny learning rate (`1e-5`) to hone micro-details on historical facades and complex stonework.
