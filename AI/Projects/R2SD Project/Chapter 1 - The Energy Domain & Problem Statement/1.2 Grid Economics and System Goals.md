## 1.2 Grid Economics and System Goals
### What This Project Solves
To integrate renewables safely, grid operators do not just need to know *what is happening now*; they need to know exactly what will happen **24 to 48 hours in advance**. 

This project builds a **Spatio-Temporal Physics-AI Engine**. It is a highly advanced forecasting system that uses meteorological weather data to predict exact power output.

### Real World Applications
With a highly accurate model, an energy manager can perform:
1.  **Energy Arbitrage:** If the AI predicts high wind tonight but low demand, the system routes the excess energy into massive Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS). Tomorrow, when wind drops and prices spike, the batteries are discharged for maximum profit.
2.  **Market Dispatch:** Solar and wind providers bid into "Day-Ahead" energy markets. If they promise 100 MW of power and fail to deliver because of a bad weather forecast, they face devastating financial penalties.
3.  **Peak Shaving:** Predicting sudden shortfalls allows the grid to trigger "demand-side response" (e.g., incentivizing factories to lower consumption temporarily) rather than turning on dirty coal plants.
