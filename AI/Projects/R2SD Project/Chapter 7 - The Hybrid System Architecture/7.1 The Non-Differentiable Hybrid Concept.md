## 7.1 The Non-Differentiable Hybrid Concept
You cannot simply attach an XGBoost tree to a PyTorch/Keras Neural Network and click "Train." 
XGBoost uses discrete math (splitting numbers). Neural Networks use continuous calculus (gradients). The gradient cannot flow backward through a tree.

**The Solution:** We implement **Sequential Training**.
1.  Train the XGBoost model to completion.
2.  Freeze it.
3.  Use it to generate base predictions on the training data.
4.  Calculate the residuals (errors).
5.  Train the Neural Network separately on those residuals.
