## Reading Order
Follow the chapters in order. Each chapter builds on the concepts from the previous one. Chapter 1 establishes the fundamental mechanics of how filters learn. Chapter 2 explains how spatial dimensions change. Chapter 3 covers downsampling. Chapter 4 ties the architecture together. Chapter 5 explores advanced components. Chapter 6 goes under the hood into training mechanics. Chapter 7 provides a practical learning path.
