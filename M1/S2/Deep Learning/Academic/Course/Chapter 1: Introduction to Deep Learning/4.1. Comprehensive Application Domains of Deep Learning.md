# 4.1. Comprehensive Application Domains of Deep Learning

Deep learning is preferred because it demonstrates unparalleled performance in image recognition, speech recognition, and natural language processing.

### 1. Transport

- **Autonomous Vehicles:** DL identifies elements in the physical environment with precision (roads, sidewalks, pedestrians, other cars).
- **Sensor Fusion:** Navigation relies on fusing multi-source data: camera images, GPS, and LiDAR point clouds to ensure safety and civil liability.

### 2. Agri-Food (Agroalimentaire)

- **Quality Control:** Visual recognition technologies identify potential debris or contaminants in food during production. The high accuracy of deep networks provides reliability that QA professionals depend on.

### 3. Security

- **Surveillance:** Object recognition is widely used to secure public spaces (airports, subways, concert halls).
- **Risk Detection:** Cameras detect risky situations such as stampedes, fights, crowd movements, or the presence of dangerous objects (weapons).

### 4. Marketing and Commerce

- **Contextualization:** Social networks use DL to identify objects, locations, people, logos, and the context of shared images.
- **Targeting:** This visual analysis allows networks to contextualize users to optimize targeted advertising.

### 5. Banking and Finance

- **Document Processing:** Automating document handling (e.g., automated check deposits via mobile apps or ATMs).
- **OCR (Optical Character Recognition):** DL reads handwritten text on checks, extracting the content to determine deposit parameters without human operator intervention.

### 6. Manufacturing Industry

- **Robotics & Logistics:** Autonomous navigation of robots in warehouses significantly accelerates the supply chain and production volume.
- **Quality Assurance:** Automated visual inspection of manufactured parts.

### 7. Medicine

- **Non-Invasive Diagnostics:** Automated analysis of medical images (X-rays, MRIs, regular photos) to diagnose pathologies like skin diseases without invasive procedures.
