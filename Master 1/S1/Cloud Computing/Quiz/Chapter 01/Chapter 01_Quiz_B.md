> [!question] On-premise vs Cloud: Which statement is correct?
> a) Cloud requires higher upfront CAPEX
> b) On-premise has lower long-term OPEX
> c) Cloud shifts CAPEX to OPEX
> d) On-premise eliminates maintenance costs
>> [!success]- Answer
