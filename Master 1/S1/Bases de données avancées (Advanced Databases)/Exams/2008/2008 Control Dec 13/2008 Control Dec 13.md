#### Exercise 1

**Statement:**
Given the following relational schema describing a real estate agency network:

*   **Agency** (agencyNum, street, city, zipCode)
*   **Staff** (staffNum, lastName, firstName, position, gender, birthDate, salary, agencyNum)
*   **PropertyForRent** (propertyNum, street, city, zipCode, type, rooms, rentAmount, ownerNum, staffNum, agencyNum)
*   **Client** (clientNum, lastName, firstName, address, phoneNum, prefType, maxRent)
*   **Owner** (ownerNum, lastName, firstName, address, phoneNum)
*   **Visit** (clientNum, propertyNum, visitDate, comment)
*   **Registration** (clientNum, agencyNum, staffNum, registrationDate)

**Questions:**

1.  Staff members whose salary is between 10,000 DA and 30,000 DA, and whose position is 'Manager' or 'Supervisor'.
2.  Number of properties that were visited in May 2004.
3.  List of all employees whose salary is greater than at least one salary of an employee from agency "A003".
4.  Find the number of properties managed by each staff member.
5.  Find all staff members who work at the Boumerdes agency (provide 2 solutions).
6.  Create a view such that the manager of agency "A003" can see details only of the staff working in their agency.
7.  Grant the SELECT privilege on the "Agency" table to all users.
8.  Promote Mr. Rabah ZHAR (StaffNum = 'EM14') to the rank of 'Manager' and raise his salary to 25,000 DA.

-- 1. Staff members whose salary is between 10,000 DA and 30,000 DA, 
-- and whose position is 'Manager' or 'Supervisor'

FROM Staff

  AND position IN ('Manager', 'Supervisor');

-- 2. Number of properties that were visited in May 2004

FROM Visit 
WHERE visitDate BETWEEN '2004-05-01' AND '2004-05-31';

-- 3. List of all employees whose salary is greater than at least one

FROM PropertyForRent
