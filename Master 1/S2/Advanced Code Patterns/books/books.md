Here is your consolidated checklist, organized specifically by **Books**, **TD/TPs (Exercises & Labs)**, and **Supports (Course Materials)**. You can use this to track your progress as you work through each resource.

---

### 1. Livres de Référence (Theory & Design Thinking)
*These books are for deep-diving into the "why" of class modeling and how to bridge the gap to code.*


- [x] **"UML @ Classroom"** – *Best for student-focused design logic and class diagram fundamentals.*
- [x] **"Object-Oriented Modeling and Design with UML" (Blaha & Rumbaugh)** – *Academic standard for design reasoning.*
- [x] **"UML Distilled" (Martin Fowler)** – *Concise, sharp, ideal for understanding associations vs. aggregations.*
- [x] **"The Elements of UML™ 2.0 Style" (Scott Ambler)** – *Best for professional conventions and clean modeling.*
- [x] **"UML 2.0 in a Nutshell"** – *Use as a technical reference for mapping UML elements to programming syntax.*

### 2. TDs & TPs (Exercises, Labs & Case Studies)
*Use these to practice the actual workflow: Read Problem → Draw Diagram → Compare with Solution.*

**University-Specific Lab Archives (The "Gold Standard"):**
- [ ] **Université de Grenoble Alpes (GL/Conception)** – *Rigorous Génie Logiciel labs.*
- [ ] **Université de Bordeaux (Conception Orientée Objet)** – *Multi-session labs with library/booking system cases.*
- [ ] **Université de Lille (UML/Génie Logiciel)** – *Complete collections of practical works.*
- [ ] **Université Paris-Saclay** – *Advanced-level, complex modeling scenarios.*
- [ ] **TD2 – Université Paris 13** – [Download PDF](https://lipn.univ-paris13.fr/~gerard/docs/corrections/uml-corr02.pdf) (Excellent for association classes/generalization).
- [ ] **TD3 – Univ. des Sciences et de la Technologie d’Oran** – [Download PDF](https://www.studocu.com/row/document/universite-des-sciences-et-de-la-technologie-doran-mohamed-boudiaf/genie-logiciel/td-3-avec-solution-td3-diagramme-de-classes-uml/50047082) (Real exam-style class diagrams).
- [ ] **TD UML – Université Mohamed V – Rabat** – [Access Document](https://www.scribd.com/document/492749386/TD-DC-Exercices-et-Corriges-1) (Practical school/personnel modeling).
- [ ] **Corrigés TD – Bliaudet Free** – [Download PDF](https://bliaudet.free.fr/IMG/pdf/TD-UML-1-2-3-4-Corriges.pdf) (Covers classes, objects, and static structure).
